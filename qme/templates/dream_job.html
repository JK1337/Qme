Find your dream job

That is the main reason to use Qme. Everything else — your profile, training, certificates, and AI tools — exists so you can discover the role you really want and get there.

After you find it, use your credits

When you have a dream job in sight (a specific posting, company, or path), you do not start from zero. You bank credits by completing play-to-learn modules and earning certificates. Those credits are what you spend on the premium moves that close the gap: tailoring your CV to that exact description, polishing your story, and sharing a sharp reQme.

In this demo, credits accrue in Play to learn; a full product would deduct credits when you run paid AI actions or exports.
